# Use cases — Business Analysts

- **Stay oriented:** Environment indicator + Classic/Fluid/Nav mode badge show where you are.
- **Jump faster:** Save Favorites for frequent components; filter and Category groups on the bar; export/import CSV to share with teammates (export warns about business keys).
- **Document pages:** Page Info copies Menu / Component / Page / ToolsRel / DB / Mode; **Copy Markdown** for tickets; includes locked field when Inspect is on.
- **Inspect fields:** Field Inspector shows Label / Rec / Fld / Row; lock copies `RECORD.FIELD` for paste into tickets.
- **Search pages:** Enable Advanced Search expand and optional Correct History in Options.
- **Quiet mode:** Skip repeated “name this environment” prompts from Options → Features.
- **Shortcuts:** Alt+Shift+P Page Info · Alt+Shift+I Inspect · Alt+Shift+C copy locked field.

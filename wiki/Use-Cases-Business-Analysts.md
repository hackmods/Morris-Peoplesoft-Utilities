# Use cases — Business Analysts

- **Stay oriented:** Environment indicator shows which PS env you’re in.
- **Jump faster:** Save Favorites for frequent components; export/import CSV to share with teammates (treat as sensitive).
- **Document pages:** Page Info copies Menu / Component / Page / ToolsRel without CTRL+J side effects.
- **Search pages:** Enable Advanced Search expand and optional Correct History in Options.
- **Quiet mode:** Skip repeated “name this environment” prompts from Options → Features.
- **Inspect fields:** Field Inspector shows record / field / row on the bar for Classic pages (hover or lock).
